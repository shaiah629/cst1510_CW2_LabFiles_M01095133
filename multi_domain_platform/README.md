## Running the Platform

1. Launch the app (e.g., via Streamlit). The platform will open in your browser.  
2. **Log in** to access the dashboard.  
3. Navigate the tabs to explore different functionalities:
   - **Analytics:** View charts and visualizations of your datasets.  
   - **Data Manager:** Insert, update, or delete dataset metadata.  
   - **AI Chatbot:** Interact with the AI-powered data science assistant.
